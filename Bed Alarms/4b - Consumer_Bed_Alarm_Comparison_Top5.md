**Guidance:**
- Consumer alarms excel at **immediate, local notification**, not analytics or escalation.
- Pressure‑pad systems generally have **lower false‑alarm rates** than motion‑only systems.
- For combined bed + chair safety at home, choose systems with **optional chair pads**.
