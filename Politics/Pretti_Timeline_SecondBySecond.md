## SECOND 0
- **Reality:** Agents are clearing civilians from the roadway as part of a federal immigration enforcement action; crowd‑control posture is evident. [9](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KtgrvzOQ3wo)  
- **DHS/CBP/Noem/Bovino:** Framed the scene as a “targeted operation” confronting a violent threat (not emphasizing crowd clearing). [1](https://www.mediamatters.org/tim-pool/tim-pool-alex-pretti-shooting-i-believe-was-suicide-cop-intentional-martyrdom)  
- **Mainstream Media:** Reported an immigration operation was underway but emphasized the **crowd‑clearing** context from video (people in the street; whistles; observers filming). [1](https://www.mediamatters.org/tim-pool/tim-pool-alex-pretti-shooting-i-believe-was-suicide-cop-intentional-martyrdom)[5](https://www.dailywire.com/news/alex-pretti-attacked-immigration-enforcement-days-before-fatal-shooting-video-shows)

## SECOND 1
- **Reality:** A woman in the roadway does not move when ordered. [9](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KtgrvzOQ3wo)  
- **DHS/CBP/Noem/Bovino:** Characterized protesters as **aggressively interfering**. [1](https://www.mediamatters.org/tim-pool/tim-pool-alex-pretti-shooting-i-believe-was-suicide-cop-intentional-martyrdom)  
- **Mainstream Media:** Described non‑movement/obstruction but not aggression, per on‑scene video. [7](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DGd87qtpPIk)

- **DHS/CBP/Noem/Bovino:** Asserted officers were responding to **danger**; initial government portrayal downplayed officer‑initiated force. [1](https://www.mediamatters.org/tim-pool/tim-pool-alex-pretti-shooting-i-believe-was-suicide-cop-intentional-martyrdom)
